About us

The Origins of Phillipe's Coffee

Welcome to Phillipe's, a coffee shop born from passion, tradition, and a love for the art of coffee brewing. Our story begins over 40 years ago in a small, bustling corner of Ghazir, where our founder, Jean Phillipe, had a dream to create a place where people could escape the fast-paced world outside and enjoy a cup of coffee brewed with care and expertise.

A Humble Beginning

In 1983, Jean Phillipe opened the first Phillipe's Café in the heart of the Latin Quarter. Inspired by the quaint cafés he grew up around, Jean wanted to create a space that felt like an extension of home — cozy, welcoming, and full of warmth. He sourced his beans from small, independent farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, forging lasting relationships with those who shared his commitment to quality and sustainability. But what truly set Phillipe’s apart was Jean’s relentless pursuit of perfection in every cup. While many cafés were focused on speed and volume, Jean's philosophy was simple: Great coffee takes time. He introduced the traditional French press, and slowly but surely, word spread about the rich, flavorful brews that could only be found at Phillipe’s.

Expanding the Legacy

As the years passed, Phillipe’s café became a beloved institution in Paris, frequented by local students, artists, and travelers alike. Each cup of coffee was brewed with love, each conversation was encouraged, and friendships blossomed over shared mugs of freshly roasted coffee. In the early 90s, Jean passed the reins to his daughter, Isabelle Phillipe, who had grown up surrounded by the aroma of roasted beans and the sounds of lively café chatter. Isabelle, who had spent years studying the art of coffee brewing and café culture, expanded the original location and introduced the world to new coffee trends, blending classic techniques with modern innovations. Phillipe’s became known not only for its exceptional coffee but also for its unique atmosphere — a place where every detail, from the vintage coffee grinders to the handmade pastries, reflected a love for craftsmanship.

Our Philosophy Today

At Phillipe’s, we honor our roots while continuing to innovate. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it’s a shared experience. From our signature blends to our seasonal offerings, every cup of coffee is a celebration of the artistry involved in bringing the beans from farm to cup. We’re proud to serve ethically sourced beans , and our commitment to sustainability remains at the forefront of everything we do. Our baristas are not just employees—they’re true coffee artisans, trained to craft each cup with the same dedication that Jean Phillipe brought to his very first café. Whether you're here to savor a quiet moment with a book, catch up with friends, or simply escape the busy world outside, we invite you to make Phillipe's your home away from home.
